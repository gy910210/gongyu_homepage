About

I'm a Director of Engineering at TikTok, leading a 35+ person team building AI Search and AI Shopping Agent for global e-commerce. I have 9+ years of experience in large-scale search and recommender systems, previously at ByteDance and Alibaba.

My work spans search, recommendation, and agent systems, which I view as different forms of large-scale decision systems for resolving user intent under uncertainty — from retrieval and ranking to multi-step reasoning and decision making.

My current research interests include LLM-powered search, LLM agents (post-training, personalization, and evaluation), and recommender systems. Recipient of the SIGIR 2017 Best Paper Honorable Mention (IRGAN, 800+ citations).

AI Shopping Agent

  • Evaluation & Benchmark — built ShoppingBench (deep product search), APeB (personalized agent behavior, with public leaderboard), and rubric-based report evaluation.
  • Impact — improved conversion rate (+0.2%), reduced query reformulation rate by 3%, and improved user engagement by +34% in side-by-side (SBS) evaluation.
